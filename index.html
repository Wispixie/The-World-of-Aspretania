History of Aspretania

The world is made of magic, and thus, all nature is the source of magic[cite: 1]. As technology progressed, humans and elves became allies and created the era of steampunk—mechanical vessels powered by magical runes and sigils[cite: 1]. Now, Elves and humans tend to live in the sky cities away from the surface dwellers, causing the rapid depletion of magic in nature as a whole and birthing more and more hollows each generation[cite: 1].
