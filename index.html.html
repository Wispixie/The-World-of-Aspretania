Lupalari

Lupalari are magical wolf-like species that are often mistaken for werewolves or wolfkin. The species operates on a tri-form biological system, shifting between a silver-haired human, a massive silver wolf, and a hulking bipedal lycanthrope. Lupalari live in mountainous regions and have deep connections with magical energy from their ancestors. They are magical purists, shunning any sort of tech, preferring to do anything by hand or magically.

History of Aspretania

The world is made of magic, and thus, all nature is the source of magic. As technology progressed, humans and elves became allies and created the era of steampunk—mechanical vessels powered by magical runes and sigils. Now, Elves and humans tend to live in the sky cities away from the surface dwellers, causing the rapid depletion of magic in nature as a whole and birthing more and more hollows each generation.

historysettingworldbuilding

Surface vs Skies

Aspretania is divided into those who live in the sky and those who live on the surface. Those in the sky thrive using elite steampunk vessels, leaving some surface areas in total darkness due to their massive infrastructure. The surface is full of exiles, criminals, and natural species who rely heavily on nature.

skysurfacelore

Magic vs Mechanisms

There are intense divides among those who prefer the old ways of magic versus the newer threat of ever-advancing technology. Hybrids of both have allowed for steampunk styled buildings and ships that traverse land and water effortlessly.

Abyssal Illness

An illness contracted within the Abyssal Forest when drinking the water. It causes someone to turn into stone slowly over a period of a month. The only known cure is cannibalism or consumption of a live species within the abyss.
